How does it all work? What is the catch?

There is no catch! Below we explain exactly how you are able to earn these digital rewards for free!

What is OPRewards.com and how do I use it?

OPRewards is a free to use online platform that connects gamers who wish to earn digital rewards for free, to companies looking to reward users for helping them out, by checking out their mobile application, watching their video advertisements, or filling out their surveys.
After you sign up for an OPRewards.com account, you are able to select from a list of different 'offers' (tasks, surveys or app downloads) to complete, provided by our partnered offer companies. When you successfully complete any offer, the partnered company will pay us money, and you will be rewarded 'points' on our website accordingly.

Why do the partnered companies pay us money?

Sometimes they need information in the form of a survey, or they want to get more downloads or players on their new game. The number of 'points' that each offer rewards, is dependent on how much money the partnered company is willing to pay for that offer to be completed. The easier offers pay less, and the harder ones usually pay more!

How are users able to earn the rewards for free?

We use the money from the offer companies to purchase digital game goods to reward to our users, keeping a small commission for ourselves! Users can instantly redeem their 'points' for the digital game goods of their choosing. This creates a fair, win-win solution for everyone involved!

Is OPRewards.com safe to use?

Definitely! We never require any private information such as your game account passwords. For some games, you will only need to provide the username of the game account you want the digital currency to be sent to. For other games, we will simply give you a gift card with a balance for you to redeem in-game.
